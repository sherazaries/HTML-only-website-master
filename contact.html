FAQ

Q. What exactly is an e-resource?
A. E-Resources and services are resources in which information is stored electronically and which are accessible through electronic systems and networks.

Q. How do they help in education?
A. In today’s world where much of the youth uses the Internet regularly, making use of e-resources in education will make students understand concepts in a manner to which they are accustomed.

Q. How can I use e-resources for teaching?
A. Pedagogically, e-resources can be used in many ways. Electronic discussion tools such as e-mail and on-line chat services can seed discussion questions before the class. Students can perform independent exercises in publishing, exhibit building, or assembling and presenting teaching units and other materials. The administration of courses can be more efficiently handled with a course home page, electronic discussion groups, and e-mail lists.
